# Report outline (Part A)

Author your report in Word (`report.docx` is your editable source; this OUTLINE.md is
only a planning aid) and submit it as `report.pdf`. Max 2,500 words (excluding
appendix and references) - you may place the required exhibits in an appendix.
Suggested structure:

1. Product and value proposition - who the user is, the gap in the market, the
   inputs the app needs.
2. Data sources and cleaning - what you loaded and how you cleaned it.
3. Integrity checks and descriptive statistics - the inventory table, the
   integrity summary, and the descriptive-stats table.
4. Return features and the text data exploration - returns/risk features and a
   descriptive look at the headlines. Do NOT score sentiment here; that is Part B.
5. What the foundation enables next.

Every figure and table must be self-contained (caption, labelled axes, units,
sample period) and referenced and interpreted in the text. The canonical list of
required exhibits is in PROJECT_BRIEF.md, Section 4.
